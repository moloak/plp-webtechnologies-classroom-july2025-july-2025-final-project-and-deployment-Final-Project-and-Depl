KEEF LAUNCHES FOUNDATION SCHOOL OF HOPE

On September 15, 2025, the Karinate Education and Empowerment Foundation proudly launched the Karinate Foundation School of Hope in Burum, Abuja — a bold step towards breaking barriers in education and empowerment.The school is a free learning center, offering primary education for children and adult education for community members who missed the opportunity earlier in life. Beyond literacy, the School of Hope is designed to equip learners with practical skills that can drive self-reliance and economic growth. Some of the hands-on training programs include:

Shower gel production, Bar soap production, Dish wash production, Disinfectant production, Marketing and business strategies. The initiative goes beyond education by creating a hub where children and adults alike can learn, grow, and thrive. By combining knowledge with vocational skills, the Karinate Foundation is empowering the Burum community with tools to combat poverty and foster sustainable livelihoods. The School of Hope stands as a testament to the foundation’s vision of transforming lives through education, skills, and empowerment — ensuring that no one is left behind.

KEEF ANNOUNCES UPCOMING VISIT TO KUJE PRISON

The Karinate Education and Empowerment Foundation continues to expand its impact with new initiatives that reach some of the most underserved groups in society. During a live broadcast on NTA, the Founder of the foundation announced that the team will be visiting Kuje Correctional Centre later this year. This initiative aligns with the foundation’s mission to bring hope, education, and empowerment to communities often overlooked. The planned visit will focus on engaging with inmates through educational programs, motivational sessions, and skills training designed to

help them reintegrate into society successfully. The announcement comes shortly after the successful launch of the Karinate Foundation School of Hope in Burum, Abuja — a free institution offering primary and adult education, alongside vocational skills training such as soap and disinfectant production, business strategies, and more. By extending its outreach to correctional facilities, the Karinate Foundation is reinforcing its commitment to restoring dignity, creating opportunities, and transforming lives across different walks of society.
